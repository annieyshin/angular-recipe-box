Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups (280 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons to 1 1/2 tablespoons (10 to 20 grams) sugar
  • 1 tablespoon (15 grams) baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon (5 grams) table salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 9 tablespoons (125 grams) chilled unsalted butter, cut into small chunks
  • 3/4 cup (175 ml) buttermilk.

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in large wide bowl
  3. Work butter into dry ingredients, add buttermilk, and stir.
  4. Transfer dough to floured counter and dispense out 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick balls of dough and place on baking sheet 2 inches apart.
  5. Bake until biscuits about 12 - 15 minutes or golden brown.
